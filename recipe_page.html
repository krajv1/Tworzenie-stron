Easy Pancake Recipe

Perfect fluffy pancakes ready in 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add milk and egg then whisk
  3. Heat lightly oiled pan
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbly
  5. Flip and cook until golden
